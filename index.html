Course Modules

In this module, we'll start by learning some background information about domestic and family violence and what our own values and assumptions are around this topic.

Here, we will look at the different forms of abuse, including physical, emotional, financial, social, and technological abuse.

This is the first step of our 3-step process. We'll learn how to spot the common signs, or "red flags," that someone may need help, especially if they reach out for help.

Next, we'll learn the "Respond" step, covering how to provide a safe, non-judgemental, and supportive response.

In this module we will look at assessing the victim's immediate needs and safety risks.

This module covers the final "Refer" step, which is all about how to guide someone to the right professional help.

Finally, we will look at the key national support services available in Australia, and what support is available if you feel you need support.
