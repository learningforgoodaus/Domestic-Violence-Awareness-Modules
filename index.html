Course Modules

In this module, we'll start by learning the official definition of domestic and family violence and understanding the common "cycle of violence."

Here, we will look at the different forms of abuse, including physical, emotional, financial, social, and technological abuse.

This module will help us challenge common misconceptions and learn the facts about domestic violence.

This is the first step of our 3-step process. We'll learn how to spot the common signs, or "red flags," that someone may need help.

Next, we'll learn the "Respond" step, covering how to provide a safe, non-judgemental, and supportive response.

This module covers the final "Refer" step, which is all about how to guide someone to the right professional help.

Finally, we will look at the key national support services available in Australia, such as 1800RESPECT, and what they do.
